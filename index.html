About D-MIX Concrete Plant

D-MIX is Djibouti's leading ready-mix concrete supplier, operating as a subsidiary of Halt Group since 2007. Our state-of-the-art batching plant produces certified concrete to international standards.

With an automated production capacity of 60 m¬≥/hour and our own quality control laboratory, we ensure every batch meets specifications. Our fleet of 15 modern mixer trucks guarantees fresh concrete delivery across Djibouti.

We serve major infrastructure, commercial, and residential projects with concrete grades from C20/25 for general construction to C40/50 for specialized applications.
