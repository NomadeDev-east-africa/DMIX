Our Story

17 Years of Concrete Excellence

Founded in 2007 as part of the prestigious Halt Group, D-MIX Concrete Plant has grown to become Djibouti's most trusted ready-mix concrete supplier. Our journey began with a simple mission: to provide the highest quality concrete for the nation's growing infrastructure needs.

Today, we operate a state-of-the-art batching plant equipped with cutting-edge technology, producing everything from standard C20/25 concrete to high-performance C40/50 grades. Our ISO 9001 certification reflects our unwavering commitment to quality and excellence.

From residential buildings to major infrastructure projects like the Grand Bara Solar Plant and Mall PK 12, we've supplied over 15,000 cubic meters of premium concrete, helping shape Djibouti's skyline and future.
